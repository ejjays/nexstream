WebM (Opus)

STUDIO MASTER

The pinnacle of digital audio. Technically the highest source quality available on YouTube servers.

  • Ultra-High Fidelity: Uses 160kbps Opus data.

M4A (AAC)

Standard

The universal everyday format. Optimized for perfect balance between size and compatibility.

  • Works on 100% of devices (iPhone, Car, TV).
  • Highly efficient 128kbps AAC stream.
