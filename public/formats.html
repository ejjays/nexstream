NexStream features a Hybrid Engine. Use our Lightning Engine for instant MP3s, or our Direct-Stream Engine for lossless M4A/WebM files that come straight from the master servers without re-encoding.

MP3 (Fast)

SPEED MODE

The fastest way to download. Optimized for instant starts and universal device support.

  • Instant: ~0.4s startup.
  • Standard 128kbps CBR quality.
  • The Tradeoff: Slight quality loss due to re-encoding.

M4A (AAC)

HQ & Compatibility

The Gold Standard. Crystal-clear audio that works on every device without losing a single bit.

  • Lossless Direct-Stream: No re-encoding.
  • Better frequency response than legacy MP3.
  • Perfect Compatibility: Works on iPhone, Android, and Cars.

WebM (Opus)

STUDIO MASTER

The pinnacle of digital audio. The highest fidelity stream available, preserved exactly from the master server.

  • Studio Grade: Captures 160kbps Opus data.
  • Preserves more high-frequency detail than M4A.
  • May require modern players (VLC, MX Player).

ðŸ”¬ Speed vs Quality: The Tech

MP3 (Speed Mode) uses Real-Time Transcoding. We decode the source and immediately re-encode it into a compatible 128kbps stream. This bypasses container checks and starts your download in under 0.5 seconds.

MP3 Engine: Source -> FFmpeg Transcode -> Client (Lightning Fast)
M4A Engine: Source -> Header Optimization -> Client (Perfect Fidelity)

Direct-Stream Copy (M4A/WebM) takes longer to initialize (~5s) because we use elite algorithms to restructure the file's "Moov Atoms" so that your Android player can read the duration and seek correctly without losing a single bit of original quality.

For instant downloads & old players, choose MP3.

For maximum quality & seeking, choose M4A or WebM.
