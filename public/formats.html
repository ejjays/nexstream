M4A (AAC)

Universal

The "Golden Standard" for modern devices. High quality and maximum compatibility.

  • Works on iPhone, Android, and Windows natively.
  • Perfect for Car Stereos & Smart TVs.
  • Supports High-Res embedded Album Art.

WebM (Opus)

Audiophile

Technically the highest quality source on YouTube. Designed for the web.

  • Superior sound clarity (160kbps Opus).
  • May not play on older Samsung devices or Car systems.
  • Limited support in some older music players.

If you aren't sure, always choose M4A.
